Who I Am

A Creative Mind with a Technical Heart

Hi! I'm Nabiha — a recent Computer Information Systems graduate from Arizona State University (W. P. Carey School of Business, Dean's Honor List 🎓) and a multifaceted creator with a passion for blending technology and imagination. I'm based in Chandler, AZ.

On the technical side, I have hands-on experience in data analysis, cybersecurity, AI tools, and full-stack development — including a real-world contract role as a Data Specialist at the City of Phoenix Fire Department. I've built AI-powered agents using Python and the OpenAI API, and I hold a Substitute Teacher Certificate for PreK–12.

On the creative side, I run five dedicated Gacha YouTube channels, write original TV and movie-like series, design digital art, and build entire fictional universes from scratch. I believe the best creators are the ones who also understand the technology behind the scenes — and I bring both to everything I do.
